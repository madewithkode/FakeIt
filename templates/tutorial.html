FAKEIT Tutorial About
FAKEIT can be used to generate fake FUTO course registration invoices, simply by inputting your courses and their desired course registration prices.

In order to make your receipts look as authentic as possible, you need to include your official passport found in your school portal. Else, a generic avatar will be used in place of your passport.

To include this passport, simply follow the following instructions:

  • Log in to your school portal
  • Press and hold your passport thumbnail
  • On the dialog box that appears, click "Open image in new tab"
  • Once the image opens in a new tab, copy the link in the address bar of the new tab
  • Finally, paste the copied link on the "Passport Link" field on the home page
FAKEIT currently supports a maximum of seven(7) courses.
